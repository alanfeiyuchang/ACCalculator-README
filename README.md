# ACCalculator

A full-screen portrait calculator for iOS with number history, date & zodiac hints, and a hidden “magic” mode.

---

## Features

- **Calculator** — Numbers, + − × ÷, with chained operations and correct precedence.
- **Number history** — Long-press on the display to see the last 10 number sets from your equations. Numbers that look like dates (e.g. 421 = Apr 21, 1203 = Dec 3) show the date and Western star sign in English and Chinese.
- **Magic mode** — Enter a number and **+**, then put the phone face down. The app computes a “magic” number (base − number on screen) and enters it digit-by-digit as you tap. The base is set in Settings (current time or a custom forced number).
- **Hidden Settings** — Swipe down with **two fingers** from the top of the screen to open Settings (tutorial and forced-number options).
- **In-app tutorial** — In Settings, open “How to use this app” for a step-by-step guide and demo video.
